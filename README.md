Meta Ad Performance Analysis Dashboard
Project Overview
Built an end-to-end analytics solution to track and evaluate the performance of paid advertising campaigns on Facebook and Instagram. The dashboard provides clear visibility into reach, engagement, conversions, and budget efficiency to help the marketing team make smarter decisions.

Business Objective
Enable the marketing team to:

Compare performance between Facebook and Instagram
Track campaign ROI and optimize budget allocation
Understand audience engagement and conversion patterns

Key Features & KPIs Delivered

Calculated core metrics: Impressions, Clicks, CTR, Engagement Rate, Conversion Rate, Purchase Rate, and Total Budget
Built interactive Power BI dashboards with filters for campaign, platform, and time period
Highlighted top-performing campaigns, audience behavior, and budget utilization insights
Automated reporting for regular performance reviews

Tools & Technologies

Power BI (Dashboards & DAX) and (Data Cleaning & Analysis)

Impact
Transformed raw Meta Ads data into clear, visual insights that support strategic marketing decisions and improve advertising ROI.
